Privacy Policy

生效日期 Effective date：2026 年 7 月 10 日

壓縮工廠在你的裝置上完成所有工作。你的照片與文件永遠不會離開你的手機——我們不收集、不傳輸、也不儲存任何個人資料。

1.不收集你的任何資料

壓縮工廠沒有帳號、不需登入、不連網路、沒有廣告，也沒有任何分析工具或第三方追蹤 SDK。你在 App 裡做的每一件事，都只發生在你的手機上，不會被記錄，也不會被傳送到任何地方。

2.一切都在你的裝置上完成

所有照片與 PDF 的壓縮都在你的裝置上進行。你選擇的檔案只在壓縮的當下於本機讀取，成品也只會存到你指定的位置——相簿、「檔案」App，或你透過分享面板選擇的地方。這些內容不會上傳到任何伺服器或雲端。

3.權限，只拿真正需要的

  • 照片：選擇照片用的是系統內建的照片選取器，所以 App 不需要、也拿不到你的完整相簿存取權。只有當你按下「存回相簿」時，才會請求「僅加入照片」權限——它只用來寫入壓縮後的成品，不會讀取你既有的照片。
  • 隱私加分：壓縮時預設會移除照片中的 GPS 位置等敏感資訊，讓你分享或上傳時更安心。

4.購買

Pro 為一次性買斷，由 Apple 的 App 內購買（StoreKit）處理。付款與交易完全由 Apple 完成，我們碰不到、也不會儲存你的付款資訊。「恢復購買」同樣透過你的 Apple 帳號由 Apple 驗證。

5.兒童

本 App 年齡分級為 4+，適合所有年齡使用，且對任何年齡的使用者都不收集資料。

6.政策變更

若本政策有任何更新，我們會同步更新本頁內容與生效日期。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Compress Factory does all its work on your device. Your photos and documents never leave your phone — we don't collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

1.None of your data is collected

Compress Factory has no account, no sign-in, no network connection, no ads, and no analytics or third-party tracking SDKs. Everything you do in the app happens only on your phone — nothing is logged, and nothing is sent anywhere.

2.Everything happens on your device

All photo and PDF compression runs on your device. The files you select are read locally only at the moment of compression, and the results are saved only where you choose — Photos, the Files app, or a destination you pick from the share sheet. Nothing is ever uploaded to any server or cloud.

3.Permissions — only what's truly needed

  • Photos: Selecting photos uses the built-in system photo picker, so the app never needs or receives access to your photo library. Only when you tap "Save to Photos" does it ask for "Add Photos Only" permission — used solely to write the compressed results, never to read your existing photos.
  • Privacy bonus: By default, compression removes sensitive information such as GPS location from your photos, so you can share and upload with confidence.

4.Purchases

Pro is a one-time purchase handled by Apple's In-App Purchase (StoreKit). Payment is processed entirely by Apple — we never see or store your payment details. "Restore Purchase" is likewise verified by Apple through your Apple Account.

5.Children

This app is rated 4+, suitable for all ages, and collects no data from anyone.

6.Changes

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page and the effective date.
