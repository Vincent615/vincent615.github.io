Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

In one line: Compress Factory is a fully on-device app. Your photos and documents are processed entirely on your device and never leave your phone. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

1.We collect no data

Compress Factory does not collect, log, or transmit any personal data. The app has no account, no sign-in, no network connection, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party tracking or data-collection SDKs.

2.Fully on-device

All photo and PDF compression happens on your device. Files you select are read locally only at the moment of compression, and the results are saved only where you choose (Photos, the Files app, or a destination you pick from the share sheet). This content is never uploaded to any server or cloud.

3.Permissions

  • Photos: Selecting photos uses the system photo picker; the app does not require or receive full photo library access. Only when you tap "Save to Photos" does it request "Add Photos Only" permission, used solely to write the compressed output — it never reads your existing library.
  • Privacy bonus: By default, compression removes sensitive metadata such as GPS location from your photos.

4.Purchases

Pro is a one-time purchase handled through Apple's In-App Purchase (StoreKit). All payment and transactions are handled by Apple; we never access or store your payment information. "Restore Purchase" is verified through your Apple Account, also handled by Apple.

5.Children

This app is rated 4+, suitable for all ages, and collects no data from users of any age.

6.Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date.
