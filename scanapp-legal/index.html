掃描工廠 Scan Factory 隱私政策

最後更新:2026 年 7 月 14 日

掃描工廠 Scan Factory 是一款文件掃描應用程式。我們的原則很簡單:你的文件是你的,我們不收集、不上傳、也看不到。

我們收集哪些資料?

不收集任何資料。掃描工廠 沒有伺服器、沒有帳號系統、不含任何分析或追蹤 SDK。開發者無法存取你的任何內容。

你的文件儲存在哪裡?

  • 裝置本機:掃描影像、辨識文字(OCR)、簽名等內容儲存在你的裝置上。
  • 你的私人 iCloud:若你已登入 iCloud,文件會同步至你 Apple 帳號的私人 iCloud 資料庫,僅你本人可存取,受 Apple 的加密與隱私保護。開發者無法讀取。

相機與照片

相機僅用於掃描文件;相簿存取僅在你主動選擇「從相簿匯入」時使用,且透過系統的照片挑選器,App 只取得你選定的照片。

Face ID / Touch ID

「文件鎖定」功能使用裝置的生物辨識驗證。驗證由 iOS 系統處理,App 不會取得也無法儲存你的生物特徵資料。

文字辨識(OCR)

文字辨識完全在裝置上執行(Apple Vision 框架),辨識內容不會離開你的裝置(除了隨你的文件同步到你的私人 iCloud)。

付費與訂閱

訂閱由 Apple 的 App Store 處理。我們不會取得你的付款資訊。你可隨時於「設定 > Apple 帳號 > 訂閱」管理或取消。

資料刪除

在 App 中刪除文件即從裝置與你的 iCloud 中移除。刪除 App 會移除所有本機資料;iCloud 中的資料可透過系統設定的 iCloud 儲存空間管理移除。

兒童隱私

掃描工廠 不收集任何人的資料,包括兒童。

政策變更

若本政策有重大變更,我們會更新本頁並修改上方的「最後更新」日期。

Scan Factory Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Scan Factory is a document scanning app. Our principle is simple: your documents are yours — we don't collect them, upload them, or see them.

What data do we collect?

None. Scan Factory has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics or tracking SDKs. The developer cannot access any of your content.

Where are your documents stored?

  • On your device: scanned images, recognized text (OCR), and signatures are stored locally.
  • In your private iCloud: if you're signed in to iCloud, documents sync to the private iCloud database of your Apple Account, accessible only to you and protected by Apple's encryption. The developer cannot read it.

Camera & Photos

The camera is used only to scan documents. Photo library access happens only when you choose "Import from Photos", via the system photo picker — the app receives only the photos you select.

Face ID / Touch ID

Document Lock uses your device's biometric authentication, handled by iOS. The app never receives or stores your biometric data.

Text Recognition (OCR)

OCR runs entirely on-device (Apple Vision framework). Recognized text never leaves your device, except as part of your documents syncing to your own private iCloud.

Payments & Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple's App Store. We never receive your payment information. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions.

Data Deletion

Deleting a document in the app removes it from your device and your iCloud. Deleting the app removes all local data; iCloud data can be removed via iCloud storage management in system Settings.

Children's Privacy

Scan Factory collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
